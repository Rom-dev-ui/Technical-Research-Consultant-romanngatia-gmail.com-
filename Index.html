About Me

Technical Research Consultant | Web, Software & Digital Products Analyst

I'm Roman Ngatia Gathoni, a research-focused technical consultant who bridges the gap between code, data, and product strategy. I combine analytical research methods with hands-on coding skills in Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to evaluate digital tools, websites, and software products from both technical and market perspectives.

As a research engineer, I specialize in identifying usability gaps, performance issues, and hidden technical risks. This includes debugging code to uncover errors, validating assumptions, analyzing competitors, and assessing scalability before products are built or scaled.

My background in electronics and PCB-level troubleshooting, combined with my ongoing Bachelor of Education in Physics and Mathematics at Mount Kenya University, gives me a unique cross-disciplinary perspective on tech-based solutions.
